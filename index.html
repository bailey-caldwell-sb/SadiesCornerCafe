About Us

Discover the story behind Sadie's Corner Cafe

A Culinary Journey

Located in the vibrant Socastee area of Myrtle Beach, Sadie's Corner Cafe has been serving the community with passion and dedication. Our team, led by Head Chef Kyle Trokey and General Manager Sarah Dixon, is committed to providing an exceptional dining experience.

We believe in using fresh, quality ingredients to create memorable meals that bring people together. Whether you're a local or visiting Myrtle Beach, we welcome you to our corner of culinary excellence.
